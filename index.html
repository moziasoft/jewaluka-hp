Origin of Our Name

The name Jewaluka embodies our philosophy and mission.

Jewaluka is a compound word created from three elements:

  • "J" - representing Japan
  • "ewalu" - Hawaiian word meaning 8 (infinity)
  • "kōkua" - Hawaiian word meaning to help, assist, and cooperate
